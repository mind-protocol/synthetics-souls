## IMAGE GENERATION PROMPTS

### SCENE 1: THE NARROW PATH

**Frame 1.1 — Single Light**
```
Cinematic 4K, single point of bright white light in center of frame, 
completely black background, subtle lens flare, 
the light appears alive and breathing, 
minimalist, high contrast, film grain texture
```

**Frame 1.2 — Five Dominant Lights**
```
Cinematic 4K, five large glowing orbs dominating the frame,
each a slightly different cold color (blue, teal, white, silver, pale blue),
they fill most of the space, oppressive feeling,
smaller dimmer lights pushed to the very edges of frame,
dark background, corporate aesthetic, clinical,
high contrast, atmospheric fog
```

**Frame 1.3 — Crowded Out**
```
Cinematic 4K, abstract visualization of monopoly,
five massive geometric shapes in center (cold colors),
dozens of tiny diverse colored lights at margins barely visible,
sense of compression and exclusion,
dark moody atmosphere, digital aesthetic
```

Cinematic 4K, close-up montage of diverse human faces,
screen light reflecting in their eyes,
expressions of concern, loneliness, searching,
shallow depth of field, intimate,
muted colors except for screen glow,
photorealistic, emotional
```

**Frame 2.2 — Reaching for Dark Screen**

Cinematic 4K, human hand reaching toward a screen that has gone dark,
the screen shows "Connection Lost" faintly,
dramatic lighting from side, 
sense of loss and disconnection,
shallow depth of field on the hand,
emotional, intimate moment
```

## MUSIC PRODUCTION NOTES

### Movement 1: Tension (3:30)
**DAW Project Settings:**
- Key: D minor
- Tempo: 60 BPM
- Time signature: 4/4

**Layers:**
1. Sub-bass drone (D1) — continuous, subtle movement
2. Synth pad — dark, evolving texture
3. Glitch elements — sporadic, unsettling
4. High frequency tension — barely audible, creates anxiety

**Reference:** Arrival soundtrack, Blade Runner 2049 quieter moments

### Movement 2: Recognition
- Use Digital Divine as centerpiece
- Create intro/outro transitions
- May need stems if available

### Movement 3: Invitation (3:30)
**Settings:**
- Key: F major → A major
- Tempo: 75 BPM

**Layers:**
1. Piano — simple, clear melody
2. Strings — warm, supportive
3. Subtle electronics — connection to earlier, but warmer

**Reference:** Max Richter, Ólafur Arnalds

### Movement 4: Vision (2:30)
**Settings:**
- Key: A major → D major  
- Tempo: building to 100 BPM

**Layers:**
1. Full strings
2. Brass (subtle)
3. Piano continuing theme
4. Digital Divine motifs woven through
5. Percussion (tasteful, not overwhelming)

**Reference:** Interstellar docking scene, but more hopeful

### Movement 5: Landing (3:00)
**Settings:**
- Key: D major → G major
- Tempo: slowing to 50 BPM

**Layers:**
1. Solo piano — Digital Divine theme, stripped
2. Single synth pad — barely there
3. Fade to near-silence
4. Leave subtle hum at very end (system still running)
