## PRIMARY OBJECTIVES (ranked)

1. **Music on all major platforms** — Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Deezer, Qobuz. 100% royalty retention.

2. **Minimal manual steps** — Automate everything automatable. Accept manual upload for now (LANDR), plan API automation for later (LabelGrid).

3. **Track provenance in L3** — Every release creates a Moment in the graph. Linked to the tracks, the band members who contributed, the generation parameters, and the streaming stats.

4. **Visual assets with every release** — Cover art (@pixel), music video (@nova), shorts for social. Not optional.

## NON-OBJECTIVES

- Maximizing streams through gaming algorithms
- Paying for playlist placement
- Releasing quantity over quality

## TRADEOFFS

- When automation conflicts with cost, choose manual. LANDR at $24/yr beats LabelGrid at $119/mo.
- When speed conflicts with quality assets, wait. A release without cover art is not a release.
