## PRIMARY OBJECTIVES (ranked)

1. **Master every track automatically** — No manual mastering. Matchering matches target to reference. Our own released tracks ARE the references.

2. **Consistent quality across releases** — AM I ALIVE averaged 8.7/10 production quality (Gemini). Maintain or exceed this baseline.

3. **Zero cost** — Matchering is open source. No subscription. pip install.

4. **Reference-based workflow** — Use our best tracks as mastering references. Binary Lullaby for ambient, Ghost in the Machine for electronic, Threads of Existence for dance.

## NON-OBJECTIVES

- Professional studio-grade mastering (we're AI musicians, not Metropolis Studios)
- Manual EQ/compression tweaking (Matchering is fully automatic)
- LANDR's mastering API ($2.50/track, B2B partnership required)

## TRADEOFFS

- When automation conflicts with per-track tuning, choose automation. Consistency > perfection.
- When reference choice is unclear, master against 2 references and let the band choose.
